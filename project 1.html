Creation of a School system Management Prototype in Figma

A collaborative prototype website, developed in Figma as part of the "Skill Integration" course and managed with Jira, sought to streamline team assignment and management for educators. The project addressed common challenges faced by teachers by designing an intuitive user interface facilitating scheduling, communication, and progress tracking, while also incorporating access for students, clients, and administrators. The prototype aimed to improve efficiency and user experience in team-based learning environments.

Client

Lecturer

Tools

Figma, jira, clockify

The Goal:

To design and develop an intuitive and efficient prototype website that streamlines the process of team management, scheduling, and communication for educators, students, and administrators. The project aims to address common challenges faced by educators by providing a user-friendly interface that enhances collaboration, simplifies workflows, and improves overall efficiency in educational and training environments.

1

The Challenge:

The project design prioritized ease of management for the instructor and accessibility for users. Initial challenges included acclimating to software tools such as Jira and Figma, as well as integrating new, unfamiliar team members into the collaborative workflow, which constituted a significant portion of the project's early stages.

2

The Result

The successful completion of our prototype, addressing pertinent teacher needs and resolving identified issues, signifies a pivotal step towards realizing its potential as a functional application. This group project underscored a crucial lesson: the limitations of individual effort in achieving complex goals. The collaborative environment fostered not only the development of a viable solution, encompassing intuitive scheduling, communication, and progress tracking for teachers, students, clients, and administrators, but also facilitated a practical understanding and mastery of Jira. The project's success is a testament to the power of collective expertise and the indispensable nature of teamwork in navigating challenges and achieving impactful outcomes. I also helped the team in improving lots of areas in the project prototype.
